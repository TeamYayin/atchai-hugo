Interactive Data Visualisation as a Service

Dataseed is a data visualisation SaaS product created by Atchai. We’ve made it simpler than ever to create beautiful, interactive data visualisations. In five minutes you can turn spreadsheets into valuable business insights. A free service is available for open data publishers, along with paid subscriptions for commercial use.

Using our open source toolkit and our REST API you can embed Dataseed into other web applications, making it easy to offer data and analytics to your customers.
